Lurker: Summerstone Mansion

Lurker is a first person thriller/puzzle game set in a steampunk Summerstone Mansion. An unnamed player needs to explore the mansion, and try to survive the night while avoiding the horrors within.

Cybernetic Revolt

Engage in a dynamic top-down shooter experience, where precision and quick reflexes are key to survival. Equipped with unique dash and shield abilities, you’ll dodge through chaos, shield against overwhelming attacks, and strategically unlock each layer of the station’s defense systems.
